Santa's Lane

I read up about this lane located in one of the neighbourhoods at 石牌, and head down to catch a glimpse. It was really heartwarming to see a community come together to put this up. Anyway, this tradition started with the church nearby and they inspiredthe families in the area to joined in the fun too.

Each family had their individual style, and most of the visitors came with their kids. I had a good time listening to the kids conversing with their parents because they were so amused by the pretty sight. Some of them even refuse to go home.

The warm atmosphere and the beautiful lights, made the night not that cold afterall.
